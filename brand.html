Typography System

Our typography emphasizes precision and clarity through lighter font weights. We avoid overly bold fonts, using thinner weights (300) for large headings to signify technical precision, while maintaining readability with appropriate weights (400) for body text and stronger weights (600) for smaller headings and labels.

Iconography Guidelines

Icon usage follows consistent principles for clarity and brand recognition.

  • Light Wave Symbolism: We use arrows with waves to signify light waves, representing the physics of our optical technology
  • Simple Icons: In small places and UI elements, we use simple, clean line-based icons for clarity and functionality
  • Realistic 3D Icons: In general, we use realistic 3D "icons" to show pops of color and precision in our imagery style, reflecting our engineering expertise
  • Size: Icons should be clearly visible, typically 24px-120px depending on context
  • Color: Icons inherit brand colors, primarily using the rainbow gradient to emphasize the spectrum of light
  • Spacing: Maintain adequate padding around icons for visual breathing room

Imagery Style

Our imagery philosophy emphasizes authentic photography that tells the story of compact precision.

  • Real Photography Only: Always use real images, not mockups or generic stock photography. Authenticity reflects our engineering integrity
  • Simplicity of Form Factor: Showcase the sleek, minimal external design of our optical systems. Emphasize compact size, clean lines, and elegant simplicity
  • Complexity Within: Reveal the sophisticated technology and intricate engineering inside such small form factors. Show detailed internals, precision components, and technical sophistication
  • The Contrast Story: These two approaches work together to tell our brand story—deceptively simple on the outside, remarkably complex on the inside
  • Color Grading: Maintain natural colors with slight emphasis on brand colors where appropriate, especially highlighting the spectrum of light in optical elements
  • Composition: Clean, modern compositions with good use of negative space to emphasize precision and clarity
