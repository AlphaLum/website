Iconography Guidelines

Icon usage follows consistent principles for clarity and brand recognition.

  • Style: Use simple, clean line-based icons or animated GIF icons for emphasis
  • Size: Icons should be clearly visible, typically 24px-120px depending on context
  • Color: Icons inherit brand colors, primarily using primary and secondary colors
  • Spacing: Maintain adequate padding around icons for visual breathing room
  • Consistency: Use the same icon style throughout the interface

Imagery Style

Guidelines for photography and illustration usage.

  • Photography: Use high-quality, professional images that showcase technology and innovation
  • Subject Matter: Focus on optical systems, AR/XR technology, and team collaboration
  • Color Grading: Maintain natural colors with slight emphasis on brand colors where appropriate
  • Composition: Clean, modern compositions with good use of negative space
  • Illustrations: Technical illustrations should be precise and informative, matching brand color palette
