Our Technologies

Ultra-efficient AR Displays

Wearable AR is fundamentally constrained by the low efficiency of transparent display technologies. Built on a decade of developments and industrialization expertise, our technology leverages Volume Phase Holograms (VPH) as optical building blocks. We enable the full spectrum of transparent displays ranging from sleek AI glasses to immersive AR. Our novel display architectures are purpose-built for efficiency, superior wearability, and cost-effective manufacturing at scale.

Learn More

Low-Power SMI Sensing

Always-on sensing in wearables and robotics is fundamentally constrained by the power and latency limits of vision-based systems. Our physics-first Self-Mixing Interferometry (SMI) technology encodes motion and distance directly into the laser signal. Enhanced by machine learning, these miniature sensors enable always-on AR eye-motion tracking and 4D motion sensing for robotics.
