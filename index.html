About AlphaLum

AlphaLum develops advanced optical solutions that drive the frontier of innovation. Leveraging decades of combined expertise in optics, electronics, software, and AI, we engineer high-performance systems built for precision, efficiency, and seamless integration.

By streamlining the path from prototype to scalable product, we help customers bring ideas to market faster, reduce development risk, and free up resources to focus on what matters mostâ€”continued innovation.

With a proven track record of bringing complex optical technologies to market, AlphaLum helps leading companies turn ideas into reliable, high-impact solutions. Our mission: to design transformative optical systems that redefine how people see, sense, and interact with the world.

Our Capabilities

Our multidisciplinary team combines expertise across multiple domains to deliver comprehensive solutions.

Holographic Systems

Next-generation AR display solutions, offering immersive experiences with a very efficient and compact form factor.

Sensing

Innovative sensing technologies engineered for next-gen applications, delivering high accuracy and rapid response in low-power, cost-effective designs.

Electronics

Custom driving solutions and integration expertise that ensure optimum performance, reliability, and seamless interfacing with advanced optical components.

Software & Hardware Co-Design

Intelligent software solutions that boost hardware performance through tight co-design with analog sensor electronics. Our integrated approach streamlines complex functions while maximizing overall system efficiency.

Artificial Intelligence

Leveraging cutting-edge EdgeAI technology, our systems deliver real-time AI/ML performance on resource-constrained devices, providing low-cost, high-performance outcomes for next-generation applications.

Advanced Optics

Cutting-edge optical systems with exceptional performance and efficiency. Leveraging innovative design principles to deliver compact and cost-effective solutions.

Manufacturing

Proven expertise in scaling from prototype to high-performance production.

Metrology

Precise solutions for characterizing, calibrating, and validating advanced optical systems with high accuracy and repeatability.

Prototyping

Rapid and precise prototyping of optical systems, enabling fast iteration, performance validation, and smooth transition to scalable production.
